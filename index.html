What is Kappa Kappa Psi?

Kappa Kappa Psi is a national honorary band service fraternity that seeks to promote and provide service for college and university bands.

Our History

The rich history of Purdue Bands and the Gamma Pi chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi is filled with years of outstanding performances and amazing adventures.

The Brothers

Despite not having a school of music, Purdue is the home of an incredible band department with many outstanding students, including the brothers of Gamma Pi.
